Milan, Italy

  1. Italian Food Highlights
    • Regional dishes vary a lot, like risotto in Milan and seafood pasta in coastal cities
    • Meals are often social and slower-paced, especially dinner with family or friends
  2. Culture and Daily Life
    • Italy is known for art, architecture, and fashion, with Milan as a major style capital
    • Public squares and cafes are important gathering places in many towns and cities
  3. Business and Economy
    • Milan is one of Italy’s main financial and business centers
    • Small family-run businesses are still a key part of the economy alongside large global brands
